Explore Your Emotional Spectrum

MoodFlix maps your mood to a curated media journey using tone, pacing, genre, and emotional intensity. The result feels personal, cinematic, and easy to browse.

How It Works

1. Mood Discovery

You answer a quick quiz so the app understands whether you want comfort, energy, focus, romance, or suspense.

2. Media Matching

We translate your answers into movie, series, and music suggestions designed to fit your current vibe.

3. Save Your Picks

Put your favorite titles into your account watchlist and return to them whenever the mood comes back.
